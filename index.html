Back from College & Ready to Coach

"A great catcher doesn't just block the ball — she commands the field, reads the game, and leads her team from behind the plate."

Whether you're just getting started or looking to sharpen your skills for the next level, EeeBee provides personalized, focused instruction built around your game.
